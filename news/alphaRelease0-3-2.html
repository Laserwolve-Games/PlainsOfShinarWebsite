updates:

  • Made the tutorial items a little easier to pick up.
  • Improved performance when generating shadows.
  • Pressing any key will exit dialog with an NPC.

bug fixes:

Fixed the following issues:

  • Moved the player back to the correct starting location. (He'd been moved up by the windmill for testing, and we forgot to move him back before releasing 0.3.1, oops.)
  • The player would still deal damage with melee attacks if the player died in the middle of casting them.
  • The player sometimes wouldn't finish their attack animation when using the basic attack.
  • Shadows weren't appearing for some entities.
  • The door to the cottage would sometimes disappear depending on the player's position.
  • Enemies with ranged attacks would still sometimes generate their projectile even if they were stunned before it appeared.
  • Applied all previous bug fixes related to the player's basic attack and abilities, to all abilities.
  • The player wouldn't rotate to face the NPC they were talking too, if they were already within talking range.
